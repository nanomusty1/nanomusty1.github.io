Privacy Policy — DailyMood

Last Updated: December 10, 2025

DailyMood is a simple mobile application designed to provide users with motivational messages. This privacy policy explains how the application handles data, the use of third-party services, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

DailyMood does not collect, store, or process any personal information from users. Specifically:

  • No account creation or login is required.
  • No user-submitted data is collected.
  • No behavioral or usage analytics are gathered.

2. Third-Party Services & Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob for displaying advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect certain technical information such as device identifiers for the purpose of serving, personalizing, and measuring ads. This data is processed by Google, not by DailyMood.

For more details, please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

DailyMood does not directly use cookies or tracking technologies. However, third-party ad networks (such as Google AdMob) may use their own tracking mechanisms according to their policies and consent requirements.

4. Children’s Privacy

The application does not knowingly collect any data from children. DailyMood is intended for general use and does not target children under the age of 13.
